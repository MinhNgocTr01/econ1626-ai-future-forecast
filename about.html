A — Methods

How the forecast was built

This is an observed trajectory, not a formal model. I did not run regressions or simulations. The method is closer to what the AI 2027 authors describe: take what is verifiable now, take the rate of change in capability and market response, and extrapolate forward to where the trajectory crosses thresholds that demand a structural response.

The strength of this approach is that it stays close to actual evidence. The weakness is that it assumes the trajectories continue. They may not. Capability curves can plateau; market responses can surprise. Where I am uncertain, I have said so in the essay's uncertainty section.

B — AI Disclosure

How I used AI in writing this

This essay was developed through extended back-and-forth with AI tools, used for brainstorming, research synthesis, structural feedback, and demonstration drafting. The final writing and all original claims are mine.

I am disclosing this as evidence, not caveat. An essay about AI's impact on writing, written with AI as a collaborator, is the most direct illustration of the ideation/execution distinction at the centre of my argument. AI helped me think. It did not think for me.

Specifically:

  • Ideation: the central instrumental/expressive distinction is mine. The four tensions (adoption vs. regret, stated vs. revealed preference, individual vs. collective rationality, visible vs. invisible displacement) emerged from conversation with the AI but the framings are mine. The choice of creative sector, the "what is a human being for" question that anchors the closing, and the decision to forecast a fork rather than a single trajectory are mine.
  • Research: AI helped me identify and verify sources. Every cited number was checked against its primary source. Where sources disagreed, I noted the disagreement rather than picking a side.
  • Structure: the timeline-driven forecast structure was developed iteratively. The decision to treat the three creative domains as evidence for a single forecast rather than parallel case studies was a turning point.
  • Drafting: early drafts went through multiple revisions with AI feedback. I rewrote each section in my own voice. The final prose is mine.

Whether the ideation/execution distinction stays meaningful, and whether the reader can tell the difference between my voice and a model's, is exactly what this forecast is about.

C — Quiz Data

About the quiz aggregates

The quiz on the landing page is illustrative, not a research instrument. The aggregate statistics shown after you complete the quiz are seeded from published research (NPR/Luminate 2026, Authors Guild 2025, Otis College 2026) rather than from real-time data collection. The site does not transmit your answers anywhere; they stay on your device.

I considered building a real backend to collect aggregate data, but the time cost outweighed the benefit for a class submission. The methods note above this paragraph is what makes the choice honest rather than concealed.
